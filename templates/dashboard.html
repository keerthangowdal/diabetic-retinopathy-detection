Diabetic Retinopathy Prediction using Fast-RCNN Classification

Abstract

Diabetic retinopathy is a leading cause of blindness, and early detection is crucial for preventing severe complications. Our platform uses a Fast-RCNN-based deep learning model to analyze retinal images and detect the presence of diabetic retinopathy in real-time. The system allows users to upload fundus images, processes them using CNN-based classification, and provides a risk assessment for diabetic retinopathy. This tool aims to assist ophthalmologists and healthcare professionals in making informed decisions for early diagnosis and treatment.
